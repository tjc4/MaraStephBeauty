Mara Stephanie Piggott, BSN, RN

Aesthetic Injector in Austin, TX

I am a nurse with a passion for injectables (Juvéderm, Restylane, Botox and Dysport).

I graduated with honors from the University of Texas at Austin's School of Nursing.  I have 13 years experience as a Registered Nurse and have spent the past 6 years in the medical aesthetics field.

In addition to my work with injectables, I am a graduate of CoolSchool and perform CoolSculpting procedures.

Before and After

Youthful Eyes

This young patient felt that her eyes looked too tired for her age and disliked the lines below her eyes.  We added 1cc Restylane to the tear trough per cannula. We followed this treatment with a series of superficial platelet-rich fibrin (PRF) injections in combination with superficial Belotero injections directly into the under eye lines to help to complete the softened look.

Cheek and Jawline Contour

This beautiful patient desired a more contoured jawline that resembled Angelina Jolie. We added 1.4cc Juvederm Voluma to the jawline 0.6cc Juvederm Voluma to lateral cheeks and 0.5cc Juvederm Ultra Plus to the chin. We also added just a touch of filler to the lips. The end result gives her more the sharpened and glamorous features that she desired.

Brow Lift & Wrinkle Reduction

This first time Botox client wanted to soften her lines while still being able to maintain movement and facial expressions.  I applied Botox to her forehead and glabella to achieve the desired result.

The top photo reveals the subtle brow lift she gets while at rest, giving her eyes an open refreshed appearance. The bottom photo shows we were able to eliminate the forehead wrinkles while retaining sufficient movement for facial expressions.
